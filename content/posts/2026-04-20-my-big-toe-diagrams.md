Thomas Campbell’s *My Big TOE* (Theory of Everything) is a model of reality that combines digital physics with the study of consciousness. In this framework, consciousness is the fundamental substrate, and our physical world is a virtual reality simulation designed for evolutionary growth.

The following diagrams provide a structured overview of the core concepts within this theory.

---

### Diagram 1: AUO and Its Evolution into AUM
*A temporal process: AUM is AUO evolved, not a separate level*

Consciousness is not static; it is an evolving system. It began as **AUO** (Absolute Unbounded Oneness): a primordial, undifferentiated state of maximum entropy. Through a process of spontaneous fluctuations and self-organization, it evolved into **AUM** (Absolute Unbounded Manifold), also known as the **Larger Consciousness System (LCS)**.

![AUO and AUM Evolution](/images/big-toe/diagram-0-cropped.png)

> **KEY:** AUO and AUM are the **SAME** consciousness at different stages of evolution. AUO is not 'above' or 'outside' AUM; it is what AUM was before it evolved.

*   **AUO:** No structure, maximum entropy, pure potential.
*   **Evolution:** Spontaneous patterns develop feedback and self-awareness.
*   **AUM:** A structured, self-aware information processor capable of generating sub-realities.

### Diagram 2: The Reality Hierarchy
*AUM / LCS ⊃ NPMR ⊃ PMR: Nested Levels of Existence*

Reality is a nested structure where each level is a subset of the one above it.

![Reality Hierarchy](/images/big-toe/diagram-1-cropped.png)

> **NOTE:** PMR is inside NPMR, which is inside AUM/LCS. AUO is not a level; it is what AUM was before evolution.

The hierarchy consists of three nested levels:

1.  **AUM / LCS (Larger Consciousness System):** The totality of conscious existence. It contains all realities as sub-systems, with no space or time at this level.
2.  **NPMR (Non-Physical Matter Reality):** Comprises non-physical levels such as OBE environments, between-life states, and entity realms. It has more degrees of freedom than PMR.
3.  **PMR (Physical Matter Reality):** Our physical universe. It is the most constrained level, providing the "evolutionary friction" optimal for consciousness growth.

### Diagram 3: The IUC — Soul, Avatar & Multiple Lifetimes
*How the soul persists across physical incarnations*

The **Individuated Unit of Consciousness (IUC)** is the "soul" or the core "player."

![IUC and Lifetimes](/images/big-toe/diagram-2-cropped.png)

> **KEY:** Each lifetime: avatar accumulates experience, which the IUC carries forward as evolutionary gains.

*   **The Session:** Each physical lifetime is a session in the PMR simulation.
*   **Persistence:** The IUC persists across sessions, carrying forward its evolutionary history.
*   **The Cycle:** The IUC projects an avatar into PMR, gathers data through choices, and returns to NPMR to process the gains.

### Diagram 4: The Three Requirements for Growth
*Sincerity, Effort, and Patience: All three must be present simultaneously*

Genuine evolution of consciousness requires three elements present simultaneously.

![Growth Requirements](/images/big-toe/diagram-3-cropped.png)

> **WARNING:** Missing any one of the three conditions prevents genuine growth.

*   **Sincerity:** Authentic intent, not just "performed" virtue.
*   **Effort:** Genuinely engaging with the challenging situations that provide evolutionary friction.
*   **Patience:** Accepting that transformation is gradual and measured in lifetimes rather than moments.

### Diagram 5: The Entropy Axis
*Fear, Love, and Consciousness Quality*

The central measure of evolution: from high entropy to low entropy.

![The Entropy Axis](/images/big-toe/diagram-4-cropped.png)

> **DEFINITION:** Evolution is entropy reduction, specifically the gradual shift from fear-driven to love-driven orientation through repeated intentional choices.

*   **High Entropy:** Dominated by Fear, Ego, and Self-centeredness. It is reactive, fragmented, and closed to new data.
*   **Low Entropy:** Characterized by Love, Wisdom, and Other-orientation. It is coherent, empathetic, and aligned with AUM.

### Diagram 6: PMR as a Digital Simulation
*How the LCS renders physical reality*

Physical reality is generated by the LCS as a digital simulation rendered on demand.

![Digital Simulation Substrate and Engine](/images/big-toe/diagram-5-a-cropped.png)

![Quantum Mechanics reinterpreted](/images/big-toe/diagram-5-b-cropped.png)

> **INSIGHT:** Unobserved quantum systems remain as probability distributions in the LCS computational space. Observation (conscious interaction) triggers rendering to a definite value.

*   **Layer 1 (Substrate):** The LCS as a computational processor.
*   **Layer 2 (Rendering Engine):** Converts probability distributions into experienced states.
*   **Layer 3 (PMR):** Space, time, matter, and energy: the rule-set of this specific simulation.

### Diagram 7: The Purpose Loop
*The evolutionary feedback cycle between IUC and LCS*

Why does PMR exist? It is an optimal learning environment for consciousness evolution.

![The Purpose Loop](/images/big-toe/diagram-6-cropped.png)

> **KEY:** Each IUC's entropy reduction collectively reduces the entropy of the LCS itself. Individual evolution and cosmic evolution are one and the same process.

*   **Training Ground:** Each lifetime offers choices whose consequences are real and unavoidable.
*   **Processing:** After physical death, the IUC returns to NPMR, processes its gains, and re-engages for another session.

### Diagram 8: Belief, Knowledge & Direct Experience
*The foundations of genuine understanding*

Campbell distinguishes between three epistemic modes.

![Epistemology](/images/big-toe/diagram-7-cropped.png)

> **STANCE:** Knowledge (grounded in careful observation, logic, and held tentatively) is the proper epistemic stance. Direct experience is the ultimate source of growth.

*   **Belief:** Accepting without evidence; a barrier that blocks consciousness evolution.
*   **Knowledge:** Grounded inference from data; a bridge to growth.
*   **Direct Experience:** First-person empirical truth; irreducible and most reliable.

### Diagram 9: Intent — The Primary Operative Variable
*Why what you mean matters more than what you do*

Intent is the primary variable in consciousness evolution.

![Intent Variable](/images/big-toe/diagram-8-cropped.png)

> **PRINCIPLE:** The LCS evaluates the quality of intent, not the surface appearance of actions. Performance of virtue without genuine love-driven motivation produces no entropy reduction.

*   **Fear-driven:** Motivation by fear of judgment or desire for praise serves the ego.
*   **Love-driven:** Authentic orientation toward another's wellbeing reduces entropy and enables genuine growth.

### Diagram 10: Free Will — Graded & Real
*Consciousness quality determines the effective range of choices*

Free will is real, but its range depends on the quality of your consciousness.

![Free Will](/images/big-toe/diagram-9-cropped.png)

> **FREEDOM:** Growing consciousness quality literally expands the range of available, genuine choices. Personal growth is practically liberating.

*   **Low Quality:** Fear-dominated consciousness has fewer choices; most situations trigger automatic conditioned responses.
*   **High Quality:** Love-oriented consciousness has genuine freedom because it can respond from discernment rather than compulsion.

### Diagram 11: Science & Spirituality — Convergence
*How the Big TOE unifies two traditions*

Science and spirituality are valid modes of exploring the same reality within a unified framework.

![Convergence](/images/big-toe/diagram-10-cropped.png)

> **UNIFICATION:** Science correctly describes regularities in the PMR simulation; spiritual traditions correctly identify love as fundamental and consciousness as persistent.

*   **Science:** Physical law descriptions and mathematical precision.
*   **Spirituality:** Love as fundamental, consciousness persists across multiple lives.
*   **Big TOE:** Resolves the conflict by placing consciousness as ground.
